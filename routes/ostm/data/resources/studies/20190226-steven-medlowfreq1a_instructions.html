You will be presented with lists of six words on the computer screen, one at a time.  At the end of the list you have to type in the words, in the order in which they were presented; i.e., the first word first, then the second word, then the third word, etc.  You must try to recall the words in the list position in which they were presented, so ff you cannot recall a word you must press "enter" to move on to the next word.

You will be presented with two practice lists and then the 32 test lists.
